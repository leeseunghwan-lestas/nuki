Tips for best results

OCR accuracy depends on the source. Keep these in mind:

Rotated or sideways text

Text rotated 90° or upside down may not be recognized correctly

Low-quality images

Very blurry or low-resolution sources reduce accuracy

Very small text

If text is tiny, zoom in on the page first before capturing

Handwriting or cursive

Handwritten text or heavily stylized cursive has lower recognition accuracy

Low contrast or busy backgrounds

Text on patterned or similar-color backgrounds may be missed

Decorative or artistic fonts

Heavily ornamented, art-style, or unusual fonts may be misread
